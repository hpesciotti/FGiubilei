Felipe Giubilei, a highly skilled and trendsetting professional in the dynamic world of audiovisuals, boasts an impressive journey. Kicked off as a Cinematographer and Editor at TV Paraíso (2010-2011), honing those essential skills.

Showed off his flair for versatility and leadership as a Cinematographer and Producer at PUC-TV Minas (2015-2016), levelling up at Escola Livre de Cinema de BH in 2016, blending theory and hands-on expertise. Thrived as a Freelance Photographer and Videographer for Cruzeiro Esporte Clube (Jan 2015 - Dec 2016). From 2017 to 2020, was behind the lens for live shows at Casa Natura Musical, seizing those historic moments.

Left his creative imprint as a Still Photographer in the 2019 feature film "SOCIALMENTE" by Leandro D'errico and as a Cinematographer in the documentary "Amazônia Samba" (TV Cultura) in the same year. Recently donned the hats of Director and Screenwriter for the trendy short film "PARALISIA" (2021), flexing his prowess in crafting and executing cinematic wonders.

Successfully connecting hands-on experience with academic brilliance, Felipe, a dedicated and versatile pro in the audiovisual scene, proudly stacks up over 100 photoshoots in his impressive portfolio.
